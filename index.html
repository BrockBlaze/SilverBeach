Silver Beach Resort
Spirit Lake Idaho

Welcome to Silver Beach Resort, part of North Idaho's best kept secret. Nestled on the shores of beautiful Spirit Lake we offer cabin rentals, boating, fishing, hiking, and swimming on our sandy beaches. Come stay with us. It's an easy drive to Coeur d'Alene, Post Falls, Spokane, and many area attractions.
