Swimming

Swim from early May into October in the warm waters of Spirit Lake. Our swim beach is clean and sandy, and has plenty of space for everyone. With 1000 feet of water frontage at Silver Beach, over half is a beautiful sandy beach with a gentle slope, designated for your swimming and frolicking pleasure. You can read a book, get a tan, watch the kids, practice your backstroke, and enjoy the peacefulness of the beautiful clear water and the mountain views surrounding us. Our beach is natural, clean, and safe for everyone's enjoyment.

Boating

Whether fishing, skiing, or just plain sight-seeing, boating is a popular sport for our guests. Boat moorage is available on site. Our convenient docks extend 200 feet out over the placid water of Spirit Lake. All guests are required to reserve dock space if bringing your boat to the lake with you. Personal watercraft users may enjoy the center of the lake and are subject to all rules applied to boats. A designated area on the beach is reserved for parking and to load and unload your passengers. Many people enjoy testing their fishing skills from the docks. Our guests can fish for perch, crappie, bass, trout, bullhead and other species.

PRICES: For Silver Beach Tenants: Boat moorage $20/day Tax included in all prices

Hiking and Biking

It doesn't matter if you are in great shape, want to go for a stroll, or need to walk the dog, you'll enjoy hiking or biking the many trails in the hundreds of acres surrounding Silver Beach. Many are groomed trails, or you can get off the beaten path to climb to the top. From there you can enjoy the awesome view of Spirit Lake and the mountains beyond. As you trek through the forest, you may see many types of wildlife and vegetation. Various trees, bushes and flowers bloom throughout the summer. The rustic town of Spirit Lake is an easy 2 mile jaunt on a paved road. Day passes for Inland Empire Paper Company may be purchased in Spirit Lake.

Fishing

Fishermen of all ages enjoy "going for the big one". Kokanee, 3 varieties of trout, Largmouth bass, crappie, perch, catfish, are all easy prey for the avid fisherman. Spirit Lake is famous for it's population of Kokanee Salmon. There are also bass, perch, crappie, trout, bullhead. Whether fishing from the docks or from your boat, you're sure to enjoy the relaxation of our wonderful lake.
