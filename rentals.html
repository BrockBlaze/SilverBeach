Cabin & RV Rentals

Throw your cares away as you relax in one of our comfortable cabins. All you need to bring is food, clothing and linens. Everything else is ready for you.

Cabin #1

This Cabin features a large open deck right on the beach. The kitchenette is equipped with a microwave, refrigerator, stove top, and dining table. The master bedroom has a double sized bed, and the loft above has two twin sized mattresses. A hide-a-bed, smart TV and DVD and a wood stove compliment the living area. Outside is a large burn pit and picnic table for memorable evenings under the moonlight. Clean rest rooms with showers are nearby.

Cabin #2

This Cabin is larger, has a cozy breakfast nook with a view of the lake. The kitchenette is equipped the same as #1. The master bedroom has a queen size bed, the loft above has two twin size mattresses. The large living area has a queen size hide-a-bed, smart TV and DVD, and lots of room to spread out. Enjoy your loved ones on the large covered deck. A fire pit and picnic table is provided for the family barbecue. Clean rest rooms with showers are nearby.
